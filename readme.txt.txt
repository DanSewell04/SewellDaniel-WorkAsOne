How to play.
 
Player 1 - Uses the keyboard
Player 2 - Uses the controller

Keyboard controller:
- WASD to move around 
- Space to jump

Gamepad Controls:
- Left stick to move around 
- X or A to jump (Depending on what controller you are using)

How to play:

- You and your partner are on a mission to escape a dungeon. 
- Coins are scattered throughout the level to allow you to unlock a door. 
- Collect the coins together to allow access to the door which takes you to the next level. 
- You can't progress without collecting all coins.
